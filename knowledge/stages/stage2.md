## Stage 2: Sentences
Ability: read basic sentences, understand S+V+O structure
Grammar: 5 basic sentence patterns, 4 tenses
Vocabulary: 1000-1500 words
Milestone: 4/5 correct on reading detail questions

Grammar details:
1. Five patterns: S+V, S+V+O, S+V+C, S+V+IO+DO, S+V+O+C
2. Simple present: I go / She goes
3. Present progressive: I am going
4. Simple past: I went (regular: -ed; irregular: go-went, do-did, have-had, see-saw, eat-ate)
5. Simple future: will go / am going to go
