## Stage 3: Grammar Framework
Ability: Identify clauses, passive voice, perfect tense
Grammar: Perfect tenses, passive voice, 3 clauses, non-finite verbs
Vocabulary: 2000-2500 words
Milestone: 60%+ on grammar single-choice

Grammar details:
1. Present perfect: have/has + done (already, yet, ever, never, since, for)
2. Past perfect: had + done
3. Passive voice: be + done (by...)
4. Attributive clause: who(人)/which(物)/that(人/物)/whose(所属)
5. Noun clause: that/what/whether
6. Adverbial clause: when/because/if/although
7. Non-finite: gerund(doing), infinitive(to do)
