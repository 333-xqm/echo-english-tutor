## Basic writing
111. Write 3 sentences about your family. -> (Sample: There are three people in my family. They are my father, my mother and me. I love my family.)
112. Write 3 sentences about your favorite animal. -> (Sample: My favorite animal is a dog. Dogs are very friendly and loyal. They can run very fast.)
113. Write 3 sentences about your school. -> (Sample: My school is big and beautiful. I like my teachers very much. I go to school by bus every day.)
114. Write 3 sentences about what you did yesterday. -> (Sample: I did my homework yesterday evening. I watched a cartoon before dinner. I went to bed at 9 o'clock.)
115. Write 3 sentences about your best friend. -> (Sample: My best friend is Tom. He is tall and kind. We play basketball together every weekend.)
116. Write 3 sentences about your daily routine. -> (Sample: I get up at 7 o'clock every morning. I have breakfast and go to school. I do my homework after dinner.)
117. Write 3 sentences about a place you like. -> (Sample: I like the park near my home. There are many trees and beautiful flowers. I go there with my parents on weekends.)
118. Write 3 sentences about your hobby. -> (Sample: I like reading books very much. I spend at least an hour reading every day. My favorite book is Harry Potter.)
119. Write 3 sentences about the weather today. -> (Sample: It is sunny and warm today. The sky is blue and clear. I want to go outside and play with my friends.)
120. Write 3-4 sentences about your dream for the future. -> (Sample: I want to become a teacher in the future. I like helping students learn new things. I will study hard to make my dream come true.)
