## Perfect tense
1. I ___ finished my HW. (have/has/had) -> have
2. She never ___ to Beijing. (has been/have been/went) -> has been
3. They ___ just had lunch. (have/has/had) -> have
4. He ___ English for 3 years. (learns/has learned/learned) -> has learned
5. I ___ seen the movie before. (have/has/had) -> have
6. She ___ already left. (has/have/had) -> has
7. We ___ been friends since 2010. (have/has/had) -> have
8. He ___ never eaten sushi. (has/have/had) -> has
9. ___ you ever been to Japan? (Have/Has/Had) -> Have
10. They ___ finished the work yet. (haven't/hasn't/hadn't) -> haven't
11. By 2020, I ___ lived here for 10 years. (have/had/has) -> had
12. She ___ graduated before she got the job. (has had) -> had

## Passive voice
13. The book ___ by Lu Xun. (wrote/was written/was writing) -> was written
14. English ___ worldwide. (speaks/is spoken/is speaking) -> is spoken
15. The house ___ last year. (built/was built/is built) -> was built
16. This song ___ by everyone. (loves/is loved/loved) -> is loved
17. The meeting ___ tomorrow. (holds/will be held/is held) -> will be held
18. Rice ___ in the south. (grows/is grown/grew) -> is grown
19. The window ___ by the boy. (broke/was broken/breaks) -> was broken
20. The letter ___ in English. (writes/was written/wrote) -> was written

## Clauses

22. I know ___ she will come. (that/what/which) -> that
23. The book ___ I bought is good. (who/which/whom) -> which
24. This is the house ___ I live. (which/where/who) -> where
25. He didn't come ___ he was ill. (but/because/so) -> because
26. The girl ___ I met is my cousin. (who/whom/which) -> whom
27. I wonder ___ he is. (that/what/who) -> who
28. ___ he said is true. (What/That/Which) -> What
29. She is the woman ___ helped me. (which/who/whom) -> who
30. I'll wait ___ you come back. (until/because/if) -> until

## Reading
The Great Wall was built by the Chinese people over 2000 years ago. It is about 21196 km long. Millions of tourists visit it every year.
46. The Great Wall was built ___ years ago. (1000/over2000/5000) -> over2000
47. The Great Wall is about ___ km. (20000/21196/30000) -> 21196
48. ___ visit it every year. (Thousands/Millions/Hundreds) -> Millions

She has learned English for 3 years. She can speak English very well now. She wants to be an English teacher in the future.
49. She learned English for ___ years. (2/3/4) -> 3
50. She wants to be a(n) ___ teacher. (Math/English/Chinese) -> English
