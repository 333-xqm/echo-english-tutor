# Chinese Learners' Top 10 Mistakes
1. Tense mixing: I go to school yesterday -> I went to school yesterday
2. Missing -s for 3rd person: He go to school -> He goes to school
3. Missing article: I have book -> I have a book
4. There be vs have: There has a book -> There is a book
5. Because + so: Because I was tired, so I slept -> Because I was tired, I slept
6. Subject-verb disagreement: The students is happy -> The students are happy
7. Wrong preposition: Depend in -> Depend on; Discuss about -> Discuss
8. Adj/adv confusion: runs quick -> runs quickly
9. Wrong relative pronoun: The book who I bought -> The book which/that I bought
10. Non-finite verb mixing: enjoy to read -> enjoy reading
