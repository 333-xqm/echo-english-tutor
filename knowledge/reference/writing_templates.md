# Writing Templates

## Letter (Letter)
Dear ___,
I am writing to you to ___.
First of all, ___. In addition, ___. Last but not least, ___.
I am looking forward to your reply.
Yours sincerely,
[Name]

## Argumentative Essay
As we all know, ___ has become a hot topic.
Different people have different opinions. Some think ___. Others argue ___.
As far as I am concerned, ___. Only in this way can we ___.
In conclusion, it is important to ___.

## Opening sentences
- I am writing to express my...
- It is well-known that...
- With the development of...
- Recently, the issue of... has aroused wide concern.

## Transition words
- First of all / To begin with
- In addition / Moreover / Furthermore
- However / On the other hand
- Therefore / As a result
- Last but not least
- In conclusion / To sum up
